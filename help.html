Getting Started

Dash Humanizer automatically transforms em-dashes (‚Äî) and optionally en-dashes (‚Äì) into ellipsis (‚Ä¶) with proper spacing, making your writing feel more natural and conversational.

Quick Start

  1. Extract the dist.zip and load it unpacked
  2. Click the extension icon in your toolbar to verify it's enabled (should show "ON")
  3. Select any text containing dashes on a webpage
  4. A bubble will appear showing the humanized version
  5. Click "Copy" or "Replace Selection" to use the transformed text

Settings

Customize the extension behavior to match your workflow. Access settings by clicking the extension icon and selecting "Settings".

Master Toggle (Action Popup)

Location: Click extension icon in toolbar

Purpose: Quickly enable or disable the entire extension

Default: ON

Auto-humanize on selection

What it does: Shows the selection bubble when you select text containing dashes

When to disable: If you find the bubble distracting or only want to use the context menu

Default: Enabled

Auto-humanize as I type/paste

What it does: Automatically transforms dashes to ellipsis as you type or paste in editable fields

When to disable: If you want manual control over transformations
